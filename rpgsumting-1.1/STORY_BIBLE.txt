RPGSumting Story Bible
======================

This file contains the current story content used by the game.
Each story node lists the scene, body text, choices, stat effects, and next destination.

Playable Jobs
-------------
Mage: Controls spells and ancient knowledge.
Starting stats: HP 80, Mana 120, Courage 4, Cunning 7

Knight: Survives danger with armor and courage.
Starting stats: HP 130, Mana 40, Courage 9, Cunning 3

Rogue: Moves quietly and wins through precision.
Starting stats: HP 95, Mana 70, Courage 6, Cunning 8

Thief: Uses tricks, speed, and risky opportunities.
Starting stats: HP 90, Mana 60, Courage 5, Cunning 10

Archer: Reads the battlefield and strikes from range.
Starting stats: HP 100, Mana 55, Courage 7, Cunning 6

Job Opening Scenarios
---------------------
Mage
----
[mage_start] Mage Prologue: The Black-Wax Summons
Scene: ACADEMY
Story: At Starfall Academy, Archmage Veyra interrupts your lesson with a royal summons sealed in black wax. The seal matches a forbidden chart of Emberdeep, the dungeon beneath Dragonspine Mountain. The chart warns that the Crown of Ash can wake Kaelthar, the oath-bound dragon below the realm.
Choices:
  1. Decode the dungeon chart before leaving
     Result: You learn the crown cannot be destroyed by force; it must be judged by an oath.
     Effects: -12 Mana, +3 Cunning, +2 Fate
     Next: hidden_library
  2. Answer the summons at once
     Result: You arrive with spellbook ready and your name already written on the quest contract.
     Effects: -4 Mana, +1 Courage, +1 Cunning, +1 Fate
     Next: tavern_call
  3. Trace the black wax with a warding spell
     Result: The wax hisses the name of the Black-Wax Covenant before burning away.
     Effects: -4 HP, -10 Mana, +1 Courage, +2 Cunning, +1 Fate
     Next: road_watch


Knight
------
[knight_start] Knight Prologue: Oath At Ashwake
Scene: CASTLE
Story: Castle Ashwake calls every sworn blade to the war room. Warden Elowen explains that the Black-Wax Covenant has opened old roads to Emberdeep and stolen the first key to the Crown of Ash. Your oath is no longer to a wall, but to every village the wall protects.
Choices:
  1. Swear to protect Greenhollow first
     Result: The captain gives you a shield marked with the roadwarden's lantern.
     Effects: +3 Courage, +2 Fate
     Next: greenhollow_square
  2. Demand a direct march on the dungeon
     Result: The veterans respect your courage, though the scouts warn that courage does not disarm traps.
     Effects: -3 HP, +3 Courage
     Next: barrow_door
  3. Question how the key was stolen
     Result: The witness describes a courtier with black wax under his fingernails.
     Effects: +1 Courage, +2 Cunning, +1 Fate
     Next: village_clues
